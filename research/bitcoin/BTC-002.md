# BTC-002: Funding Rate Research

**Phase:** 2 (Bitcoin — Second Phase)
**Status:** Planning
**Objective:** Test whether perpetual swap funding rates contain predictive information for future BTC returns.

---

## Research Question

Do Bitcoin perpetual swap funding rates (the periodic payments between long and short positions) predict future returns? Funding rates reflect the relative demand for long vs short leverage — extremely positive funding implies crowded longs, potentially signaling a top.

## Dataset

| Source | Symbol | Coverage | Frequency |
|--------|--------|----------|-----------|
| Gate.io API | BTC_USDT perpetual | 2019-11-18 to 2026-06-08 | 8-hour (capped at ±0.375%) |
| Yahoo Finance | BTC-USD | 2014-09-17 to 2026-06-08 | Daily OHLCV |

Funding rate data is merged onto daily BTC-USD data by resampling to daily (mean funding rate per day).

## Hypotheses

### H1: Funding Rate Extremes → Return Reversal
When funding is extremely positive (longs paying shorts → crowded long), shorts are forced to pay less, suggesting potential downside. When funding is extremely negative, the opposite.

- **Signals:** Top/bottom 5% and 1% of daily funding rate
- **Horizons:** 1d, 2d, 5d, 10d, 20d

### H2: Funding Rate Level (Quintiles)
Does the quintile of daily funding rate predict future returns?

- **Signals:** Quintile membership
- **Horizons:** 1d, 2d, 5d, 10d, 20d

### H3: Funding Rate Change (Δ)
Do sudden shifts in funding rate predict short-term returns?

- **Signals:** Top/bottom quintile and 5% of 1-day and 3-day change in funding rate
- **Horizons:** 1d, 2d, 5d

### H4: Cumulative Funding Pressure
Does accumulated funding over the past N days predict returns?

- **Signals:** Top/bottom quintile of rolling N-day sum of funding rates
- **Windows:** 5d, 10d, 20d, 60d
- **Horizons:** 1d, 5d, 10d, 20d

### H5: Funding Regime Persistence
When funding has been persistently positive/negative for extended periods, does it predict a regime change?

- **Signals:** Consecutive positive/negative funding days (streaks of 3, 5, 7, 10)
- **Horizons:** 1d, 5d, 10d

## Validation Protocol

Standard framework validation (matching BTC-001A and Gold protocol):

| Test | Description | Standard |
|------|-------------|----------|
| T1 | Basic screening | p<0.05, SR>1.0, PF>1.30, N>50 |
| T2 | Walk-forward | 3/4 temporal periods positive |
| T3 | Out-of-sample | Test SR>0, PF>1.0, decay<30% |
| T4 | Multiple testing | BH FDR q<0.05 |
| T5 | Monte Carlo | p<0.05 (5,000 permutations) |
| T6 | Drift neutralization | Alpha > 0 vs buy-and-hold |

## Expected Outcome

Unlike price structure signals, funding rates measure market positioning directly. If extreme funding predicts reversals, this would represent a genuinely different source of alpha from BTC-001A.

- If edges found: document for replication
- If no edges survive: Bitcoin classified as efficient under free data
